Our Story

Serving Happiness. One Scoop at a Time.

At Funday Creamery, we believe ice cream is more than just dessert — it's an emotion, a memory, a celebration. Whether it's a scorching summer afternoon, a joyful weekend outing, or just a mid-week craving for something sweet, we're here to make your moments a little cooler and a lot more fun.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Born to Spread Smiles

Founded by a bunch of dessert dreamers and flavour fanatics, Funday Creamery was born out of a simple idea — to make people happy, one scoop at a time. We started with a tiny counter and a big mission: craft delicious, clean treats that spark joy.

Today, we're growing fast — with outlets popping up across cities, events buzzing with Funday carts, and a growing tribe of fans who believe in fun without compromise.

📍 Local, Lovable, and Always Fresh

Every store is designed to be a mini escape. Bright, cheerful, and Insta-ready — our spaces are just like our ice creams: playful, bold, and hard to resist.

We're proudly local — rooted in our communities, sourcing local produce, and crafting every flavour fresh in small batches. No shortcuts, no artificial flavors, no weird chemicals. Just real ingredients and real fun.
